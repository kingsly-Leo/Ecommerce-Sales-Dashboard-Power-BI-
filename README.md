# Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze key metrics in an e-commerce business. It enables stakeholders to understand sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and revenue patterns through clear visual insights and dynamic filters.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance and supports data-driven decision-making for marketing, supply chain, and sales operations.

The Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is designed to answer crucial business questions such as:

- **Which products are generating the highest revenue?**
- **What is the monthly and yearly sales trend?**
- **Which regions contribute most to total sales?**
- **What is the overall profit/loss situation?**
- **How do customer segments behave across categories?**

Interactive slicers allow stakeholders to explore data by region, period, product category, and customer type.
